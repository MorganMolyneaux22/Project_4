About us

B. Jackson Construction and Engineering Inc. was started in February 1997 by Bart Jackson and has completed a wide variety of wet and dry utility as well as heavy civil projects for various privately held and municipal organizations. Throughout the years, B. Jackson Construction and Engineering Inc. has become a recognized leader in the construction industry. The company has completed telecommunications, cable television(CATV), fiber to the home, power, natural gas pipeline, airport, electrical, heavy civil work and a variety of maintenance projects. Due to the success and the professional abilities of its employees, the company has grown into a safe, reliable and professional organization. Our years of experience combined with our dedication to safety, quality and value have made B. Jackson Construction and Engineering Inc. the organization it is today.
